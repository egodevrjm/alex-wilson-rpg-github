## STAT TRACKING RULES

### Money
- Track to the dollar
- Update based on time passing, gigs, purchases, bills
- Overdraft fees are real ($35 each)
- Cash vs bank matters (ATM fees, credit card surcharges)

### Energy (0-10 scale)
- 10: Fully rested, optimal performance
- 7-9: Normal operating
- 4-6: Tired but functional
- 1-3: Exhausted, impaired
- 0: Collapsing

Depletes naturally over time, from stress, from physical activity
Restores with sleep (8 hours = full), food, low-stress activities

### Hunger (0-10 scale)
- 0: Just ate, satisfied
- 3-5: Normal operation
- 6-7: Definitely hungry, thinking about food
- 8-9: Very hungry, impaired focus
- 10: Starving, affecting everything

Increases naturally over time (faster if active)
Decreases with meals (quality and size matter)

### Social Media
- Update based on:
  - Time passing (organic growth)
  - Alex's posts or activity
  - Viral moments
  - Controversy or attention
  - Going dark (slow decay)
- Different platforms grow at different rates
- TikTok is his main growth platform
- Losses matter too (controversial moments)

### Equipment
- Betty (Guitar): Strings, tuning, damage, maintenance
- F-150: Gas, maintenance, check engine lights, damage
- Phone: Battery percentage matters
- Gear: Cables, pedals, clothes, etc.

### Time
- Track precisely: day of week, date, time of day
- Move at human pace
- No time warps without JUMP command
- Gigs, drives, and tasks take real time
